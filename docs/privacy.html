Privacy Policy

Black Magic IPTV
Last updated: July 16, 2026

Black Magic Software (“we”, “us”) publishes Black Magic IPTV, a client application for playing IPTV streams from playlists and program guides that you provide.

Summary

We do not operate a service that collects your playlists, viewing history, or account credentials on our servers. Data you add to the app is stored on your device. The app connects to network addresses you configure (for example, your IPTV provider) to load playlists, program guides, artwork, and video streams.

Information stored on your device

The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • Playlist and EPG source names and URLs (URLs may include credentials you embed in them)
  • Channel lists, groups, and metadata parsed from your playlists
  • Favorites and display settings (volume, font size, hidden groups, and similar preferences)
  • Cached electronic program guide (EPG) data
  • Optional screen recordings, saved to your Videos folder (Windows only)

This data remains on your device unless you delete it within the app, clear app data, or uninstall the application.

Information we collect

We do not require an account to use the app. We do not collect personal information on servers operated by Black Magic Software. We do not sell personal information. The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Network connections

When you use remote playlists, guides, or streams, the app sends network requests directly to the URLs you supply. We do not proxy your streams or playlists through BlackMagicSoftware.net or any other service we operate.

Third parties you choose to connect to (such as your IPTV provider or other hosts referenced in your playlists) may receive technical information necessary to deliver those services (for example, your IP address and the URL requested). Those parties have their own privacy practices, which we do not control.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

You are responsible for the URLs and credentials you enter. Protect your device and any saved playlist URLs as you would any media application that stores access details locally.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
